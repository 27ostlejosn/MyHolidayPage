Thanksgiving Dinner

Thanksgiving dinner is the highlight of thanksgiving. It's what people love about it. But what if you want to spice it up a bit more this year? Well you can incorporate in some other traditions from other cultures into your thanksgiving this year.

Some Thanksgiving dinner traditions include:

  1. Breaking the wish bone for good luck
  2. Watch or play an American Football Game
  3. Run a turkey trot
  4. Family-Loved Foods
